Joined Code Nation

In 2019 I had just finished Sixth Form and was abit confused with what I wanted to do next. I didn't want to go to uni and i was struggling to get a job so my Uncle introduced my to Code Nation and it changed my life. It was an amazing experience and I got to be around people whop shared the same passion and love for coding as I did, I was there for 12 weeks in an intense master course and at the end I had learnt so much more in the coding area and was proud to be able to call myself a Junior Developer. I left Code Nation with a wider knowledge of coding, new friends I still talk to to this day but sadly no job.
